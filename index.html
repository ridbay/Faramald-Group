Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer international shipping?
Yes, Faramald Farms exports High Quality Cassava Flour and Starch to international markets. We handle all necessary export documentation to ensure seamless delivery.
What is the minimum order quantity (MOQ)?
For our industrial partners, the MOQ is 1 Ton. For retail distributors of Faramald Garri, please contact our sales team for bulk pricing bundles.
Are your products certified?
Absolutely. Our processing facility adheres to NAFDAC regulations and international food safety standards to ensure 100% purity and hygiene.
Can I visit the farm or factory?
We welcome industrial partners and investors for facility tours by appointment. Please use the contact form to schedule a visit with our management team.
